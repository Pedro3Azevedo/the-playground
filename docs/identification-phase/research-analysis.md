
# Survey

- 79% of the responses are from men.
- The majority of the responses are form people from 18 to 35 years old.
- The majority is from Lisbon, where it was more distributed a focus.
- The majority works full time and then are students form university.
- Almost everyone uses public courts, where some also play in private/payed courts
- Almost everyone started to play basketball before 17 years old.

- There are some types of players, the majority goes with friends to play against other players, ones play against each other, and there are others that go alone to shoot some balls and open to play games.
- 58% would like to have more competition in these games.
- Besides the court and weather conditions, some people complain about other players, how they don't take it seriously, or they are too aggressive or are individualists.
  Also, complain about exclusivity in games, and not knowing how many people are in the court
